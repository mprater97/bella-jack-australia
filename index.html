Circus & Aerial

NICA (National Institute of Circus Arts) in Brunswick — the best in Australia. Aerial silks, lyra rings, trapeze, tumbling. Cirque du Soleil scouts visit here. Youth programs from age 8+. Circus Oz runs youth workshops in Collingwood. Train where the pros train.

🛍️

Shopping

Chadstone — biggest shopping centre in the Southern Hemisphere. 550+ stores. Zara, H&M, Uniqlo, JD Sports, Cotton On, Sportsgirl. Chapel Street South Yarra for indie fashion. Melbourne Central with the iconic shot tower. DFO South Wharf for outlets. Late night shopping Thursday + Friday.

class="c">🧋

Bubble Tea & Chinese Food

Gong Cha, CoCo, ShareTea, Tiger Sugar, Gotcha — literally every block in the CBD. Box Hill — entire Asian food district. HuTong dumplings, Haidilao hotpot, Shark Fin House yum cha. Chinatown on Little Bourke Street — oldest in the Southern Hemisphere. Glen Waverley for Korean and Japanese. You will never run out of places to eat.

Cute Animals

Healesville Sanctuary — hand-feed kangaroos, see platypus, meet wombats. Phillip Island Penguin Parade — hundreds of penguins waddle up the beach every sunset. Moonlit Sanctuary — night tours with possums and owls. Wild kangaroos at Anglesea. Koalas in trees along the Great Otway National Park.

Music & Culture

Australia's live music capital. Hosier Lane and AC/DC Lane for street art. Federation Square for free events. Queen Victoria Night Market every Wednesday in summer. Moomba Festival, White Night, Comedy Festival. Greville Street Prahran for record shops and vintage.

Skateparks

Best in the world — and free. Frankston Skatepark — huge bowls, street section, rails. Riverslide on the Yarra River. Waterways, Box Hill, Geelong Waterfront by the beach. Mornington on the coast. All year round. No ice. No rain. Just ride.

& VR

Zero Latency North Melbourne — world's first free-roam VR. Walk around shooting zombies with mates. Fortress Melbourne — biggest esports venue in Australia. PAX Australia every October. VR arcades in Chadstone and Highpoint. Same Xbox account, same Minecraft worlds, same friends online.

Trampolining & Circus

Bounce Inc Glen Iris — freestyle areas, foam pits, dodgeball, slam dunk. Sky Zone Heatherton. NICA for circus — trapeze, tumbling, acrobatics. Melbourne Gymnastics Centre for competitive trampolining.

Beach Days

Friday after school — 10 min to Frankston Beach. Golden sand, calm water, foreshore cafes. Brighton Beach with iconic bathing boxes. Half Moon Bay for snorkelling. Weekends at Torquay or Lorne on the Great Ocean Road.

🌡️

The Weather

Scotland: 185 rainy days. Dark by 3pm in winter. Melbourne: 230+ sunny days. Winter is 10–15°C (Scottish summer). Summer 25–35°C. Sun sets 8:30pm. Kids play outside until dark. BBQs in the garden.

🦘

Wildlife

Wild kangaroos at Anglesea and Wilsons Prom. Koalas along the Great Ocean Road. Penguins at Phillip Island every sunset. Dolphins at Sorrento. Rainbow lorikeets in your garden. Wombats. Platypus at Healesville.

🏡

Our House

Open-plan living flowing to outdoor decks. Proper gardens. Built-in BBQs. Many homes have pools. Even a modest rental has more space than most UK houses. Room to breathe.
